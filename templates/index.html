Improved Visibility

Kogii is a light. But it improves safety by responding and reacting to improve your visibility.

Live Awareness

Kogii comes with an Android app. If desired, mount your phone to your handlebars for additional awareness.

Variable Light

Kogii constantly monitors it's surroundings. When it detects vehicles, the light will dynamically flash to enchance visibility.

SafetyGauge

In real time, the Kogii Android app will inform you of the level of danger on a specific road based on historical data.

Making The Roads A Safer Place

Kogii monitors proximal and GPS data as you cycle. We use that data to analyse
and predict dangerous roads. Our goal is to improve road safety for everyone.
